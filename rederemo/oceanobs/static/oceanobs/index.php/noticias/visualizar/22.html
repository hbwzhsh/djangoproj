REMO participates in the organization of the first International Workshop on Operational Oceanography for Developing Countries, hosted Beijing, China, 2013

Fonte: REMO

REMO, represented by Drs. Clemente A. S. Tanajura e Mauro Cirano, was part of the scientific committee that organized the first International Workshop on Operational Oceanography for Developing Countries. The event was held in Beijing, China, during September 9-12th, 2013. The workshop was part of the 12th Chinese Academy of Sciences - The World Academy of Sciences - World Meteorological Organization (CAS-TWAS-WMO) Forum and was lead by Prof. Jiang Zhu, director of the Institute of Atmospheric Sciences (IAP/CAS). The scientific committee was coordinated by Prof. Qingcun Zeng and Prof. Jiang Zhu (IAP/CAS) and was composed by 8 scientists representing China, Brazil, India and South Africa. The workshop had the participation of 48 researchers, representing 8 countries (China, India, Brazil, South Africa, Chile, Indonesia, Nigeria e Kenya). Brazil was represented by 10 researchers (5 of them associated to REMO). Brazilian institutions that participated in the event included: the National Institute for Space Research (Dr. Luciano P. Pezzi), the Federal University of Bahia (Drs. Clemente A. S. Tanajura e Mauro Cirano, MsC Leonardo N. Lima and Davi Mignac), the Federal University of Rio Grande (Drs. Osmar O. Möller and Maurício M. Mata), the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (Dr. Afonso M. Paiva) and the University of São Paulo (Drs. Edmo J. D. Campos e Ricardo de Camargo). Several ongoing activities in operational oceanography lead by the participating countries were presented, some with strong international collaboration. A more detailed description of the event will be submitted for publication at both the EOS/AGU and the BAMS/AMS and a special issue comprising the most relevant works will be published in Atmospheric and Oceanic Science Letters. It is expected that this Forum will gain permanent character by the realization of joint work and regular meetings.
