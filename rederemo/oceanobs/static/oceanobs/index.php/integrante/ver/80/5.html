Graduated in Oceanography from the Federal University of Rio Grande (1991), MSc in Physical Oceanography at the Oceanographic Institute of the University of São Paulo (1995) and Ph.D. in Physical Oceanography from the University of New South Wales, Sydney, Australia (2000). Since 2004 he has a permanent position at the Federal University of Bahia, currently holding the position of Associate Professor I. In 2011 took a sabbatical at the South Australian Research and Development Institute (SARDI), Adelaide, Australia. His areas of research interest include the study of circulation processes in coastal and oceanic, both based on the analysis of oceanographic data, and through numerical modeling of circulation. Since 2006 he coordinates the regional modelling efforts of REMO. He is a member of the Coastal Ocean and Shelf Seas Task Team (COSS-TT), linked to GODAE OceanView.
